About Us

What is The Resorts Main Goal?

Inclusive Luxury spiritual retreat located in beautiful Zephyr, California. Here at the Pacific Trails Resort we want you to Find Your Inner Peace Relax an escape from the outside world that will leave you Refreshed and brand new.

How Do we Do This?

We help you with beautiful scenery and activities that will connect you to the Universe. Enjoy the outdoors from woodland hikes to the coast of the beach! We are well known for our diverse plant and animal life, terrific weather, and bringing people tranquility and peace.

Our founder

Christy Cole started with a dream from enjoying the local scenery to ownership of the property with the gift of sharing the resort to everyone. A Californian with the goal to bring euphoria, relaxation and peace of mind to all visitors. It is common to feel stuck in a world of work, this is a escape of your daily life you will be ready and motivated to strive on and do your best!
